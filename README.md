## ✨ Features

- 🎥 **Video Background** on the hero section  
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** design (mobile, tablet, desktop)  
- 📂 **Projects Showcase** with hover animations  
- 👤 **About Me Section** with detailed info  
- 💡 **Skills Section** showing all technologies  
- 🎨 **Smooth AOS Animations**  
- 🔗 **Mobile Navigation Menu** with slide-in effect  
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** (HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
- **JavaScript**
- **AOS (Animate On Scroll)**
- **Boxicons**
- Video Background Integration

## 💡 Usage

- Showcase your personal projects, skills, and blog posts.

- The contact form can be connected to your preferred email service or backend.
